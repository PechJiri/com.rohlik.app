The app supports multiple Rohlik regions:
Czech Republic: https://www.rohlik.cz (default)
Germany: https://www.knuspr.de
Austria: https://www.gurkerl.at
Hungary: https://www.kifli.hu
Romania: https://www.sezamo.ro

App allows you to control your cart (adding or removing items), get info about your upcoming orders and delivery slots.

Testing on CZ region.
